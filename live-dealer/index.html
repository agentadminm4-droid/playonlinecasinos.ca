What Are Live Dealer Games?

Live dealer games stream real human dealers from professional studios directly to your device. You place bets digitally, but the cards, roulette wheel, or dice are physical — dealt in real-time by trained croupiers. It's the closest thing to a physical casino experience you can get online.

Why Play Live Dealer?

  • Real cards and wheels — no random number generator. You see the shuffle, the deal, the spin.
  • Live chat with dealers — ask questions, tip, banter. It's a social experience.
  • Higher bet limits — many live tables accept $500-$5,000+ per hand.
  • Game-show variants — Crazy Time, Monopoly Live, Lightning Roulette — formats that don't exist in physical casinos.
