Who We Are

PlayOnlineCasinos.ca is an independent guide to live dealer games, online slots, and table games at AGCO-licensed Ontario casinos. We're a small team of gaming industry analysts based in Ontario.

How We Test Casinos

Every casino we recommend goes through a 50-point review process. We test:

  • AGCO license verification against the iGaming Ontario public registry
  • Real-money deposit and withdrawal (we put our own money in)
  • Game library breadth and live dealer quality
  • Mobile performance on iOS and Android
  • Customer support responsiveness (we send real questions)
  • Bonus terms and conditions (we read the fine print)

Our Editorial Standards

We do not accept payment in exchange for positive coverage. Our team has no financial interest in any specific operator. If a casino is AGCO-licensed and meets our quality bar, we recommend it. If it doesn't, we don't — regardless of affiliate revenue.

Compliance

All operators on this site are licensed by the Alcohol and Gaming Commission of Ontario (AGCO) and regulated by iGaming Ontario. We verify this before any operator is listed. You must be 19+ and physically located in Ontario to play.

Responsible Gambling

Gambling should be fun. If it's not, help is available. See our Responsible Gambling page for resources including ConnexOntario (1-866-531-2600), PlaySense, and the Responsible Gambling Council.
