Game Overview

Evolution Live Blackjack uses 8 decks shuffled continuously by an automatic shuffler. The game follows standard Vegas Strip rules: dealer stands on all 17s, blackjack pays 3:2, and you can double down on any two cards (including after split).

Bet range varies by casino, but most Ontario operators offer $5-$5,000 per hand. VIP tables go higher. Side bets are optional and pay independently of the main hand.

Side Bets

Perfect Pairs: Pays if your first two cards are a pair. Mixed pair (one red, one black) pays 6:1, colored pair (same color, different suit) pays 12:1, perfect pair (same suit) pays 25:1. House edge ~5-7%.

21+3: Pays if your two cards plus the dealer's upcard form a poker hand. Flush pays 5:1, straight 10:1, three of a kind 30:1, straight flush 40:1, suited three of a kind 100:1. House edge ~5%.

Side bets are fun but the RTP is significantly lower than main game. Stick to the main bet if you're using basic strategy.

Basic Strategy

With perfect basic strategy, Evolution Live Blackjack has an RTP of 99.5% — one of the highest in any casino game. Key rules:

  • Always stand on hard 17+
  • Hit hard 11 or less
  • Stand on soft 19+
  • Double down on hard 10-11 when dealer shows 2-9
  • Split 8s and Aces always
  • Never take insurance (house edge ~7%)

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I count cards in live blackjack?
Technically yes, but Evolution uses continuous shuffle machines (CSMs) that reshuffle the discards back into the shoe after each round. This makes traditional card counting ineffective — there's no deck penetration to exploit. The house edge remains 0.5% with perfect basic strategy regardless of counting attempts.
What's the minimum bet?
Minimum bets vary by casino and table. Most Ontario casinos offer $5 minimums on standard tables and $25-$50 on VIP tables. Some high-roller Salon Privé tables start at $1,000.
Can I play for free?
No. Evolution Live Blackjack is real-money only. If you want to practice basic strategy, try a free RNG blackjack game first, then move to live dealer when you're ready for real stakes.
