Game Basics

Sweet Bonanza is a 6-reel, pay-anywhere slot with a candy/fruit theme. Wins are formed by landing 8+ matching symbols anywhere on the reels — no paylines required. The tumble mechanic removes winning symbols and drops new ones, creating chain-reaction wins from a single spin.

Bet range is $0.20 to $100 per spin at most Ontario casinos. The Ante Bet feature doubles the chance of triggering free spins at 25% extra cost.

Free Spins Feature

Four or more lollipop scatters trigger 10 free spins. During free spins, multiplier bomb symbols appear with values from 2x to 100x. When a multiplier bomb lands as part of a winning tumble, it multiplies the win. Multiple multipliers add together.

The combination of unlimited retriggers and high-value multipliers is what gives Sweet Bonanza its 21,100x max win potential. A 100x bomb landing on a full-screen premium symbol tumble is the dream scenario.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Sweet Bonanza high volatility?
Yes. Sweet Bonanza is rated high volatility, meaning long dry spells with occasional big wins. The max win is 21,100x your bet, but most sessions won't come close. Bankroll discipline is essential.
What is the Ante Bet?
The Ante Bet costs 25% extra per spin and doubles your chance of triggering the free spins feature. With Ante Bet on, the free spins trigger rate is roughly 1 in 90 spins (vs 1 in 180 without). The RTP remains 96.48% in both modes.
Can I buy the bonus on Sweet Bonanza?
