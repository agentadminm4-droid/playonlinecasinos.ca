Game Variants

Evolution offers several live roulette variants in Ontario:

  • European Roulette: Single zero, 97.3% RTP. The standard and best-value option.
  • American Roulette: Double zero, 94.7% RTP. Avoid if European is available.
  • Lightning Roulette: European base game with random multipliers (50x-500x) on straight-up numbers. Same 97.3% RTP but higher variance.
  • Immersive Roulette: European base game with multiple camera angles and slow-motion replays. Pure visual experience.
  • Speed Roulette: European base game with rounds every 25 seconds. Same RTP, faster pace.
  • Auto Roulette: No dealer, fully automated. European base game, 97.3% RTP.

Betting Options

Standard inside/outside bets are available on all variants. Outside bets (red/black, odd/even, high/low) pay 1:1 with ~48.6% chance on European. Inside bets (single numbers, splits, corners) pay higher with lower probability. Lightning Roulette adds a 50x-500x multiplier to 1-5 random straight-up numbers each round — but the straight-up bet RTP drops to ~83% to fund the multipliers.

Strategy Notes

Roulette is a game of pure chance — no strategy changes the long-term RTP. The best you can do is play European (97.3% RTP) instead of American (94.7% RTP) and avoid the 5-number bet (0,00,1,2,3) which has a 7.9% house edge on American wheels.

Popular systems like Martingale, Fibonacci, and D'Alembert don't change the math — they just change the bet sizing pattern. The house edge is the same on every spin regardless of bet size or pattern.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best live roulette variant?
European Roulette is the best for RTP (97.3%). Lightning Roulette has the same base RTP but adds variance through multipliers — fun for big-win seekers but worse for steady play.
Can I use a roulette system to beat the game?
No betting system changes the long-term house edge. Martingale, Fibonacci, and similar systems only change the pattern of wins and losses, not the math. Stick to flat bets and play European.
What is the maximum payout?
Standard straight-up bet pays 35:1. Lightning Roulette multipliers can boost straight-up wins to 500:1, but the RTP on that specific bet drops accordingly.
