Game Basics

Mega Moolah is a 5-reel, 25-payline slot with an African safari theme. The base game is straightforward: match symbols across paylines for wins. The real action is in the Jackpot Bonus Wheel feature, triggered randomly by any spin.

Bet range is $0.25 to $6.25 per spin at most Ontario casinos. The jackpot bonus can trigger on any bet size, but the chance of hitting the Mega Jackpot is higher with larger bets.

Progressive Jackpots

Mega Moolah has four progressive jackpots:

  • Mini: Starting at $10. Triggers frequently (~every few hours across the network).
  • Minor: Starting at $100. Triggers every few days.
  • Major: Starting at $10,000. Triggers every few weeks.
  • Mega: Starting at $1,000,000. Has paid out up to €19.4 million.

The jackpots grow continuously until won, then reset to the seed amount. The Mega Jackpot typically pays out every 4-8 weeks.

RTP and Variance

Mega Moolah has a base RTP of 88.12% — significantly lower than most slots. This is because a portion of every bet funds the progressive jackpots. The math changes if you hit a jackpot, of course, but most players won't.

Volatility is medium. Base game wins are frequent and modest. The big wins come from the Jackpot Bonus Wheel or the Free Spins feature (which can retrigger indefinitely with 5x multipliers).
